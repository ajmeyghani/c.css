Padding and Margins

Below is a list of helper classes for setting padding and margings for elements.

Padding

.pad: 1rem
.pad--relaxed: 2rem
.pt: padding top 1rem
.pb: padding bottom 1rem
.pbt: padding top and bottom 1rem
.pl: padding left 1rem
.pr: padding right 1rem
.plf: padding left right 1rem

Margin

Similar to padding helper classes, there are also helper classes for margins. There are:

  • .mt: Margin top for 1rem
  • .mb: Margin bottom for 1rem
  • .mbt: Margin top and bottom for 1rem
  • .margin: Margin around for 1rem
